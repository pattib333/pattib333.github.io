What is a Cat Café?

Cat cafés had their origin in Taiwan, with one opening in Taipei in 1998. The café became popular with Japanese tourists as well as local visitors, who enjoyed the opportunity to interact with some furry friends.

KitTea was the first cat café to announce its plans to open in the United States. Although KitTea was unable to open as the first cat café due to raised rents and construction delays, they are proudly known as the first and only cat café in San Francisco, California. On June 24, 2015, KitTea finally opened its doors to the anticipating public and continues to support cats from Wonder Cat Rescue and the ACC in San Francisco.

Meet our Kitties!

Buster

Buster is a playful guy, he is always pulling pranks on people! Despite his age, he is very intelligent and savvy. Buster would make a great addition to a home with kids.

Chester

Chester is not very outgoing. Once he trusts you, he won't leave you alone. Chester comes from a single-parent home; an elderly lady who has recently moved away.

Cleo

Cleo is all about style. If his fur is ruffled, you can bet he will take a timeout from play to get it straight! Cleo is looking for a family of his own.

Hugh

Hugh can be tempermental at times, but is generally kind and playful.

Jersey

Jersey is the boss, and all is well as long as you keep this in mind. Jersey always seeks the highest spot in the room, to ensure that all is well in his domain.

John (Wayne)

John Wayne is a fearless and pugnacious kitty. He will challenge even the toughest of competitors, but can be friendly and warm.

Marshal

Marshal is as scatterbrained as he looks! He has a friendly demeanor but seems to enjoy chasing his tail more than anything.
