Why Pet Photography?

I have loved animals all of my life - their funny ways, their different personalities, and their fierce loyalty. When you have a pet, you have a friend for life! Unfortunately, they are taken from us much too quickly. My passion is to capture these precious moments – to celebrate new arrivals into your family; to preserve memories of a love that is like no other. I strive to capture your pet’s personality, and your pet's interaction with your family.

Important Notes

  • You are responsible for paying all permit, parking or access fees associated with the shooting location.
  • Each photo shoot should last between 1-1/2 and 3 hours, except for the Quick and Simple package.
  • Each package includes the sitting fee for 1-2 animals and 1-2 humans. More can be added for a fee; we must agree beforehand on number of subjects.

Prints

  • If you wish, I can touch up any prints for an additional fee.
