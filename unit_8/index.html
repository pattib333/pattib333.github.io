Why Pet Photography?

Capture your memories of your precious one now, so they can be with you forever.

From my lifelong love of animals, I have come to the realization that our precious pets are taken from us much too quickly.

My passion is to capture those precious moments that you share with your pet – to preserve memories of a love that is like no other. I strive to capture your pet’s personality, and his/her interaction with you and your family.

Important Notes

  • Travel fees apply for locations more than 30 miles from my home.
  • You are responsible for paying all permit, parking or access fees associated with the shooting location.
  • Each photo shoot should last between 1-1/2 and 3 hours, except for the Quick and Simple package.
  • Each package includes the sitting fee for 1-2 animals and 1-2 humans. More can be added for a fee; we must agree beforehand on number of subjects.
  • A 50% deposit is required at the beginning of the photo shoot.

Prints

  • You will have access to all digital images taken within 1 week of the photo shoot. They are yours to share with family, friends, upload to social media, whatever you choose to do with them.
  • If you wish, I can touch up any prints for an additional fee.
  • Printed prints are available for a fee.
